My Books

Financial Modelling - Let's Master the Basics

- by Varun

 A comprehensive guide perfect for beginners who want to learn the fundamentals of financial modelling and gain a deeper understanding of the key concepts and principles that underlie financial modelling.
 This book covers everything you need to know about financial modelling, including the basics of financial statements, forecasting techniques, and the different types of financial models. You'll also learn how to use Excel to build models and analyze data, making the book an ideal resource for those looking to improve their Excel skills.
 What sets "Financial Modelling - Let's Master the Basics" apart is its practical approach to teaching. Rather than focusing solely on theory, the book provides step-by-step instructions and real-world examples to help you build your own financial models and gain confidence in your abilities.

Financial Modelling - Let's Start with the Basics

- by Varun

Are you eager to acquire a thorough understanding of financial modelling, but unsure where to begin? Look no further than "Financial Modelling - Let's Start With The Basics"! This comprehensive guide is perfect for novices seeking to develop a firm grasp of financial modelling concepts and techniques.
This book delves into the fundamentals of financial modelling, including an overview of financial statements, vital financial ratios, forecasting methodologies, and the different types of financial models. Moreover, you'll gain valuable insights into using Excel to construct models and scrutinize data, making this book an essential resource for elevating your Excel proficiency.

Who am I?

 My name is Varun and I am a finance professional based in India with a deep love for numbers. Professionally, I am entrusted with the oversight and management of the Financial Modelling and Valuations team at SG Analytics. In this role, I am responsible for implementing strategic initiatives, project management, and client relationship management.

 I liaise closely with various prominent institutions, such as Investment Banks, Private Equity firms, Venture Capital firms, Corporates, Startups, and Valuation Advisory firms, to assist them with their financial assignments, including advanced financial modelling, business valuations, FP&A, and Quality of Earnings, to name a few.

 Previously, I have worked with Moody's Analytics and TresVista Analytics in a similar capacity. My achievements in this field have been recognized through several awards, including the 'Gem Award' and ‘Spot Award’ at SG Analytics and the 'Rising Star' title at Moody's Analytics, for exhibiting a steep learning curve and contributing to the growth of the business.

 I have a passion for sharing my experience and knowledge with young minds to help them acquire and excel in financial skills. I lead the Finance Learning Department at IIM Skills and am responsible for setting the right techniques, the best industry-relevant curriculum, and content delivery. Additionally, I deliver guest sessions at various educational institutions and corporate offices on financial modelling, financial analysis, business valuation, and related topics.

 I have also authored two books, 'Financial Modelling - Let's Start with the Basics' and 'Financial Modelling - Let's Master the Basics', in my endeavor to impart financial knowledge to even the most remote parts of society.

 I hold a management degree from the University of Delhi and have cleared all five modules for CFP certification, successfully earned a CFA Institute Investment Foundations certificate and am pursuing the CFA program. I am also passionate about entrepreneurship and founded 'NETOPP: Network of Opportunities', a web-based platform for the latest news and events happening in major universities and college campuses across Delhi NCR, in 2016. I ran the venture successfully for one year and have since mentored entrepreneurs to onboard strong financial vision and unravel financial needs.

 Apart from my professional and entrepreneurial pursuits, I am actively involved in social services. I have served as the founding President of the Rotaract Club of Young Visionaries, VP at Soul of Life NGO, and Head at Dressing the Souls NGO, among others.
